Meet the Team

Below are the bios of the great members of our team! Each member is certified and experienced in helping players of all skill levels reach their goals! Feel free to reach out to any of the team members directly through the contact page!

Jared Brungard - Head Coach / Founder

Jared is an emerging Pro and has made multiple APP and PPA main draws. He has won multiple Nataional Championships on the College and Amatuer Rankings. He has a 5.5 DUPR and is a certifed Teaching Professional

Liam Duffin - Lead Instructor

Liam in a multiple time All American Pickleball Player for Utah Tech University. He has had multiple main draw wins in singles, mixed and mens doubles. He has won both individual and team national Championships and has been a lead pro at mutiple pickleball facilities.

Jack Munro - Professional Consultant

If you dont know Jack, you dont know pro pickleball. He has been a preiemer MLP player, countless medals on the APP tour, and also a college national champion of the Univeristy of Texas. We welcome him to our team as a consultant.
